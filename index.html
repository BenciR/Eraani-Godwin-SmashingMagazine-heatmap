Person of the week

Hui Jing Chen is a self-taught designer and developer who launched her web design career during downtime between training sessions when she was a full-time basketball player. Today, she is a UX Developer at Shopify and a member of the CSS Working Group. To bring together the CSS community in Singapore, Hui Jing co-founded the Talk.CSS meetup five years ago. She also shares her knowledge and her passion for CSS as a speaker and writer. You can find Hui Jing on Twitter as @hj_chen. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Hui Jing!
